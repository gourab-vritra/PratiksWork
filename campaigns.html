Youth Knowledge Development Program

Youth Knowledge Development Program is a innovative approach taken by Sristy. 20 Schools, 300 Students , 1 Trophy. Different Schools from Ranaghat Sub division are participating in different events like Singing, Drawing , Quiz, Drama, Extempo. School that wins most number of prices can be the "Champion of Championes". So, come and join us to make your school proud.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Schools from Ranaghat Sub divion can participate.
  • Students from class V - XII can be part of this awesome events.
  • There is no restriction on the number of students participating from a particular school.
  • There is no entry fee for the participating Schools.

YKDP Rules

  • There is multiple events in YKDP most likely Drawing , Singing , Dancing , Quiz, Drama . But events type can be changed by Authority.
  • In every events there may be two group i.e Group- A and Group- B. Gruop A - Class V-VIII , Group B- IX-XII.
  • In every events there is prices for every 1st , 2nd and 3rd Place holder. This is applicable for each group.

Champion Of Championes

Join us in the epic battle of Champion Of Championes. The school that wins most number of prices in different events will be the Champion Of Championes. So , prepare now to make your effort count and let your school feel proud of you.

Are you passionate about Quiz , Drawing , Singing or Dancing ? Are you toooo good as an organiser ? Then this is the right place for you. Come , join and bring out the most awesome organisor from you.
