Ranaghat Naba Sristy Welfare Society

Established on 5th August at Ranaghat....

“Ranaghat Naba Sristy Welfare Society was established on 5th August , 2012 at Ranaghat Courtmore . Starting with 10 members now it has 25 members working together to bring change in the world . ”

Ranaghat Naba Sristy Welfare Society is a Govt regd Organisation(Regd No. S/L/4189). This is non profit organisation . The moto of this Social organisation is to help the needy people , do good for environment . With a little financial support still Sristy is going good.

Social and Cultural Sides of Sristy.....

“Ranaghat Naba Sristy is working with Social and cultural aspects also. Every year we organise a Youth Knowledge Development Programmme that provide students to explore their talents before a wide range of Audience;“

Ranaghat Naba Sristy teach it's volunteer to work for the society . We also give value to the cultural part of the society . Members of Ranaghat Naba Sristy are alwasy dedicated to serve the society and people .
